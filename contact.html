Frequently Asked Questions

How does the "Pay As You Wish" system work?

Simply enjoy your coffee, then pay what you feel is fair based on your experience. We trust our community to value our work honestly.

Do you accept reservations?

We operate on a walk-in basis to maintain our casual, welcoming atmosphere. However, for groups of 10+, please contact us in advance.

Is WiFi available?

Yes! We provide free high-speed WiFi for all our guests. Perfect for remote work or casual browsing.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We can provide coffee catering for events, meetings, and special occasions. Contact us for details and pricing.
